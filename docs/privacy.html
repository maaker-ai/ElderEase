ElderEase Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

ElderEase is a medication reminder app that prioritizes your privacy. All your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

ElderEase does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All medication records, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal identification information
  • No health or medication data
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers

Local Notifications

ElderEase uses local notifications to remind you about medications. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server.

In-App Purchases

ElderEase offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock unlimited features. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase verification. RevenueCat may receive anonymized transaction data from Apple. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is RevenueCat for in-app purchase management. No other third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks are included.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by uninstalling ElderEase. No data remains on any server after deletion.

Children's Privacy

ElderEase does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any account creation or personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
